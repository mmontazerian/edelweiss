My passion is to join clients on their journey of SELF DISCOVERY.

Saba Montazerian is a Licensed Marriage and Family Therapist (LMFT) who has extensive experience working with individuals, couples and families in various therapeutic settings to include outpatient, intensive-in-home/crisis counseling, and Therapeutic-Day Treatment programs with individuals of all ages.  She graduated with a Master’s of Arts degree in Marriage and Family Therapy from Adler University, Chicago, and also obtained a Master’s of Science degree in Clinical Psychology from Sussex University, England.

Saba specializes in working with individuals with mood disorders, anxiety, AD/HD, attachment issues, trauma, grief, family conflict and parent-child relationships, parenting strategies, couples issues, identity and acculturation issues, as well as common life stressors and transitions.
